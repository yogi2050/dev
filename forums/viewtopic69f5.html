Hi guys, It’s time to give something back to this wonderful community. If you have a brand new website, you can follow these steps to do SEO for it:

SEO Checklist: (0-1 month old site)

a) On-page SEO. (keyword research…etc)
b) Submit your site to the popular search engines (this is the first step of off-page SEO)
c) 1 Social Bookmark a day (only in trusted SB Sites like digg,reddit etc…)
d) 5 Directory Submissions a day
e) 2 forum profiles built per day
f) 2 Article Submissions per week (3 article directories per submission)
g) 1 Web 2.0 Property creation per week.
h) 2 Blog Directory Submission per day
i) 2 RSS Feed Submission per day
j) 2 blog commenting per week.
k) 1 Press Release per month
